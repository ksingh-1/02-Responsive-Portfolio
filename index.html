I am a Software Developer located in Oklahoma with a background in hospitality management and Psychology looking to make the change into Tech and Software Development!

My fondest memories in life have been about cars and creating something that is unique, special and your own. I see technology and software very close to similar as working on cars. You can create something from scratch or work with a team to improve existing code to optimize it to be better. When you don't find me coding I'll be working in the garage fixing, replacing or upgrading cars, trucks, motorcycles. My long term goal is to work on technology and software that will be used in cars and trucks. My background in hotels lets me work and talk with people and understand their perspectives on what direction our technology is going. The outlook is positive in this industry, I want to contribute to that positivity!
